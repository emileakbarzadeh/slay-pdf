Sign PDF without uploading FAQ

Can I sign a PDF without uploading it?

You can add a visual signature, initials, dates and text in the browser, then export the edited PDF.

Is this a certificate-based e-signature?

No. Slay PDF places visual marks on the document. It does not provide certificate-based e-signature workflows.
