Slay PDF gives you page thumbnails, selection controls and split markers so you can break apart a large PDF without first handing it to a remote service.

Free online PDF splitter FAQ

Can I split a PDF online for free?

Yes. Slay PDF can split or extract PDF pages in the browser without an account.

Does the splitter upload my PDF?

No. The PDF is processed locally in your browser.

Can I create more than one PDF from the same file?

Yes. Add split markers between sections and download the separated PDFs.
