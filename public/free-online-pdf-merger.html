Is Slay PDF a free online PDF merger?

Yes. It runs in the browser and can merge PDF pages without an account or subscription.

Are my PDFs uploaded while merging?

No. Slay PDF processes the merge in your browser instead of uploading source PDFs to an application server.

Can I merge selected pages only?

Yes. Select the pages you want or delete pages you do not need before exporting.
