Local workspace

Download results

Export all pages, selected pages, separate PDFs, images, text or encrypted PDFs from the local workspace.

Load it before you need it.

For the best offline experience, open Slay PDF while online first so the browser can cache the app and its editing assets. After that, many common workflows are local browser work.

Offline behavior still depends on the browser, cache state and device storage. For sensitive work, test your needed workflow before relying on it away from a connection.

Offline PDF editor FAQ

Does Slay PDF work offline?

Slay PDF is offline-capable after the app and browser assets have been loaded and cached by your browser.

Do PDFs upload when I work offline?

No. Offline work happens in the local browser workspace and cannot upload documents to Slay PDF.
