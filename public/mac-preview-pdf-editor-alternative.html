Why use Slay PDF instead?

Preview is useful for simple viewing and markup. Slay PDF gives you a broader local workspace for PDF page organization, redaction, OCR, compression and export.

Use it when a file needs cleanup before sending, filing or printing and you want the work to stay in the browser.

Mac Preview PDF editor alternative FAQ

Is Slay PDF a replacement for Preview?

No. Slay PDF is a separate browser-based PDF editor for jobs that need more page and export tools.

Can Slay PDF merge and split PDFs on Mac?

Yes. Import PDFs, reorder pages, add split markers and export merged or separated PDFs locally.

Does Slay PDF work without uploading the file?

Yes. Documents and edits stay in this browser, and passwords are never saved.
