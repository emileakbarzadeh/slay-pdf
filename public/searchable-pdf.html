Can Slay PDF make a scanned PDF searchable?

Yes. Slay PDF can run local English OCR and export searchable PDF files.

Can I clean pages before OCR?

Yes. Rotate, crop, delete or reorder pages before exporting.

Does OCR require uploading the PDF?

No. Common OCR work runs locally in this browser.
