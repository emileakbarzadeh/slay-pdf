Adobe Acrobat alternative

A local PDF editor for jobs that should not need Acrobat.

Slay PDF covers everyday PDF editing in a browser: merge pages, split documents, sign PDFs, resize pages, posterise prints, annotate, redact and export.

No account wall

Open the app, import a PDF and get the edit done.

Local by default

Your files stay in the browser rather than being uploaded to a conversion service.

Practical exports

Download all pages, selected pages, separate PDFs, images, text or searchable OCR PDFs.

Use Acrobat when you need Acrobat. Use Slay PDF for the rest.

Some enterprise workflows need Adobe's full document platform. Many everyday PDF jobs do not. Slay PDF is aimed at the quick edits people usually need from a PDF tool without the overhead.

Because it is a static client-side app, it is also a better fit when you care more about keeping files local than syncing documents into another service.
