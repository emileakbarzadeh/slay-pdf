Acrobat Pro alternative

A local Acrobat Pro alternative for everyday PDF edits.

Slay PDF covers the common edit, organize, sign, OCR and export jobs that should not require a heavyweight subscription workflow.

Common edits

Merge, split, delete, rotate, crop, resize, annotate, sign and watermark PDFs in one browser workspace.

Export tools

Download all pages, selected pages, separate PDFs, page images, extracted text or searchable OCR output.

Local processing

Documents stay in this browser while you work instead of being uploaded to a Slay PDF app server.

For PDF jobs that do not need enterprise Acrobat.

Use Slay PDF when the task is practical document work: assemble files, remove pages, add a signature, crop a scan, redact visible areas, compress an export or make a scanned document searchable.

It is not a full Acrobat Pro clone, certificate signing platform or document management suite. It is a fast local editor for common PDF jobs.

Acrobat Pro alternative FAQ

Can Slay PDF replace Acrobat Pro?

Slay PDF can replace many lightweight edit, organize, sign, OCR and export jobs. It is not a full enterprise Acrobat Pro replacement.

Does Slay PDF require a subscription?

No. Slay PDF is a free local browser app and does not require a Slay PDF subscription.
