Resize PDF without uploading FAQ

Can I resize a PDF without uploading it?

Yes. Open the PDF in Slay PDF, choose a new page size and export the resized file locally.

Will resizing stretch the PDF?

No. Slay PDF scales the existing page content into the new size and uses white letterboxing when needed.
