Yes. Use Slay PDF in the browser to crop pages and export the updated PDF.

Will cropping upload my document?

No. The working PDF stays in the browser during editing and export.

Can I crop only part of a PDF?

Yes. Select the pages you want to adjust before applying crop or resize settings.
