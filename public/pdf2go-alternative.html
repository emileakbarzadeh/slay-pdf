Why use Slay PDF instead?

Slay PDF keeps common PDF jobs in one local workspace: page organization, signing, redaction, compression, OCR and export. It is designed for quick document work where uploading to a remote utility site is unnecessary.

It works best for everyday files such as receipts, forms, scans, statements, packets and approval copies.

PDF2Go alternative FAQ

Is Slay PDF a PDF2Go clone?

No. Slay PDF is an independent local PDF editor focused on browser-side PDF editing and export workflows.

Does Slay PDF upload documents to edit them?

No. Your documents and edits stay in this browser. Passwords are never saved.

Does Slay PDF add a watermark?

No. Slay PDF exports clean PDFs without adding a Slay PDF watermark.

Slay PDF is independent and not affiliated with PDF2Go.
