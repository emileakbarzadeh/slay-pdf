Combining PDFs often means receipts, contracts, scans or client files. Slay PDF keeps the everyday merge workflow in the browser so those files do not need to enter another upload queue.

Combine PDF FAQ

Can Slay PDF combine multiple PDFs?

Yes. Import multiple PDFs, reorder the pages and export the combined result.

Can I combine images with PDFs?

Yes. Slay PDF can import common image formats and include them in the exported PDF.

Are files uploaded to combine PDFs?

No. Common combine and export workflows run locally in this browser.
