For lightweight signing, not enterprise e-sign.

Slay PDF is a practical fit when a document needs a visible signature mark or filled form fields before sending. It is not a certificate-based signing or identity verification platform.

The benefit is speed and locality: import the file, add the visible mark or form edits, and download the finished PDF without uploading it to Slay PDF.

Fill and sign alternative FAQ

Can Slay PDF replace Adobe Fill and Sign?

Slay PDF can replace lightweight visual signature and supported form-filling jobs. It is not a certificate-based e-signature platform.

Does Slay PDF save signatures or passwords?

Documents and edits stay in the browser, and passwords are never saved.
