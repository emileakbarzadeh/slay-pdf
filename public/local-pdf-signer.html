Local PDF signer FAQ

Can I sign a PDF without uploading it?

Yes. Slay PDF lets you place signatures in the browser and export the result locally.

Can I move a signature after placing it?

Yes. Select the signature item in the editor, then move or resize it before exporting.

Is this a legal e-signature service?

No. Slay PDF edits the PDF file locally. For workflows requiring identity verification, audit trails or legal signing compliance, use the required signing platform.
