Free PDF editor

Edit PDFs locally in your browser.

Slay PDF is a free Adobe Acrobat alternative for everyday PDF edits: merge files, split pages, delete pages, sign documents, resize pages and export clean PDFs without uploading anything.

Private by design

Your PDF data stays in the browser. Documents are not sent to an app server.

Useful tools

Merge, split, reorder, delete, rotate, crop, resize, sign, annotate and posterise PDFs.

Installable app

Run it as a static web app and keep recent local workspaces in browser storage.

A PDF editor for quick work, not account walls.

Slay PDF focuses on the tasks people usually need immediately: combining pages, removing pages, signing a document, exporting selected pages, adding simple annotations and downloading a finished PDF.

Because the app runs locally, it is a practical choice for private PDFs, client documents, forms and one-off edits where uploading to a random converter site is not acceptable.
