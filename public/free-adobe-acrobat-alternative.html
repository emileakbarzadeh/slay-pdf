Free Adobe Acrobat alternative FAQ

No. It replaces common local editing workflows, not every enterprise Acrobat feature.

Can I sign documents?

Yes. Slay PDF supports visual signatures and annotation marks for everyday signing workflows.

Can I protect exports with a password?

Yes. Password-protected exports are available, and passwords are never saved.
