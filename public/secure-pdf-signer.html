It is not a certified e-signature platform. If a workflow requires identity verification, audit trails or legal signing compliance, use the required signing provider.

Secure PDF signer FAQ

Can I sign a PDF without uploading it?

Yes. Slay PDF places signature edits in the browser and exports a local signed PDF.

Can I adjust a signature before export?

Yes. Select the signature item to move, resize or remove it before download.

Is this a certified e-signature service?

No. Slay PDF edits the PDF locally. Use a compliant signing platform when audit trails or identity verification are required.
