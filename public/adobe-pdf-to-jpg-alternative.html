Adobe PDF to JPG alternative FAQ

Can Slay PDF export PDF pages as images?

Yes. Use page image export to download PDF pages as local image files from the browser workspace.

Do PDF-to-image exports upload the PDF?

No. The export workflow runs in your browser instead of uploading the source PDF to Slay PDF servers.
