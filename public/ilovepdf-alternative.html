iLovePDF alternative FAQ

Does Slay PDF upload PDFs to process them?

No. Slay PDF is designed around local browser processing, so documents are not sent to a Slay PDF app server.

Can I use Slay PDF for merge and split jobs?

Yes. Import documents, reorder pages, add split markers or export selected pages directly from the browser.
