Slay PDF is for practical contract cleanup: combining attachments, removing extra pages, marking a copy and exporting selected pages.

Visual signing is for placing marks on a PDF. It is not a certificate-based e-signature or contract management system.

Contract PDF editor FAQ

Can I sign a contract PDF in Slay PDF?

You can add a visual signature, initials, dates and text to a PDF. Slay PDF does not provide certificate-based e-signature workflows.

Can I redact contract details locally?

Yes. Add redaction blocks and export a rasterized redacted copy so the covered page content is flattened into the output.
