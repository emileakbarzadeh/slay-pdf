Highlight PDF FAQ

Can I highlight a PDF in Slay PDF?

Yes. Open a page in the editor and add highlight overlays before exporting.

Can I add notes too?

Yes. Add text overlays, rectangles and ink drawings where needed.

Does Slay PDF upload highlighted PDFs?

No. Common editing and export workflows run locally in this browser.
