Merge PDF online FAQ

Can I merge PDF files online for free?

Yes. Slay PDF can merge PDF pages in the browser without an account, subscription or watermark workflow.

Are my PDFs uploaded while merging?

No. The source PDFs stay in this browser instead of being uploaded to a Slay PDF application server.

Can I remove pages before exporting?

Yes. Delete unwanted pages and reorder the rest before downloading the merged PDF.
