When Slay PDF makes sense

Use Slay PDF when you need a fast PDF edit and do not want a heavyweight document platform involved: combine files, pull out pages, remove scans, add a signature, redact a copy, resize pages or export selected pages.

Use Acrobat when you need Adobe's full enterprise document workflows, collaboration stack or advanced platform features. Slay PDF is for the local jobs that should be quick.

Adobe Acrobat vs Slay PDF FAQ

Which is better for quick local PDF edits?

Slay PDF is built for quick local edits such as merge, split, delete, reorder, sign visually, crop, resize and export selected pages.

Which is better for enterprise document workflows?

Adobe Acrobat is the better fit for enterprise document management, organization workflows and advanced platform features.
