Local PDF OCR FAQ

Can I OCR a PDF locally?

Yes. Slay PDF can run OCR from the browser and export a searchable PDF.

Should I rotate scans before OCR?

Yes. Correcting sideways pages first usually gives OCR a better page image to read.

Are passwords saved during OCR?

No. Passwords are never saved.
