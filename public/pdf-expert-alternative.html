Why use Slay PDF instead?

Slay PDF is a good fit when you only need the practical parts of a PDF editor: organize pages, mark up a copy, sign a file, redact details or export a cleaned PDF.

It is not a full replacement for every native app workflow, but it avoids a lot of friction for everyday documents.

PDF Expert alternative FAQ

Is Slay PDF a PDF Expert clone?

No. Slay PDF is an independent browser-based PDF editor for common local editing workflows.

Can I use Slay PDF on a Mac?

Yes. Use a current browser on macOS for local PDF editing, export and OCR workflows.

Does Slay PDF upload my PDF to edit it?

No. Your documents and edits stay in this browser. Passwords are never saved.
