Smallpdf alternative FAQ

Is Slay PDF a Smallpdf clone?

No. Slay PDF is an independent local PDF editor focused on browser-side PDF editing and export workflows.

Do I need an account to use Slay PDF?

No. You can open the editor, import documents and export PDFs without creating a Slay PDF account.
