Draw on PDF FAQ

Can I draw on a PDF?

Yes. Use the ink tool in the page editor to add freehand marks.

Can I move or resize edits?

Yes. Select overlays in the editor to move or scale them before export.

Does drawing on a PDF require signup?

No. Common local PDF workflows do not require a Slay PDF account.
