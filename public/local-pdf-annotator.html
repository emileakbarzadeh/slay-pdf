Local PDF annotator FAQ

Can I annotate a PDF locally?

Yes. Open the PDF in Slay PDF, add page edits and export the finished document from the browser.

Can I move annotations after adding them?

Yes. Select edits on the page to reposition, resize or delete them.

Can I sign PDFs this way?

Yes. Use the local signing and edit tools to place a signature before exporting.
