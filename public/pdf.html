PDF tools FAQ

What can I do with Slay PDF?

You can merge, split, sign, annotate, redact, rotate, crop, resize, OCR, extract text and export PDFs locally.

Is Slay PDF only for one PDF at a time?

No. You can import multiple PDFs and image files into the same workspace.

Do I need an account?

No. Common Slay PDF workflows do not require an account.
