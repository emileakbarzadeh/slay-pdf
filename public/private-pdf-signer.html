Sign everyday documents without a remote portal.

Slay PDF is built for lightweight private signing jobs such as approvals, school forms, internal acknowledgements, rental paperwork and scanned documents.

It edits the PDF file locally. For signing workflows that require verified identity, audit trails or compliance guarantees, use the required signing provider.

Private PDF signer FAQ

Can I sign a PDF privately?

Yes. Slay PDF lets you place signature edits in the browser and export the result locally.

Can I move a signature after placing it?

Yes. Select the signature item, then move, resize or remove it before export.

Is this a full e-signature platform?

No. Slay PDF edits PDFs locally. Use a compliant signing platform when identity verification or audit trails are required.
