Acrobat Online alternative

Use a local browser PDF editor instead.

Slay PDF opens like an online tool, but common PDF processing happens in your browser: merge, split, sign, compress, OCR and export.

Browser convenience

Open the app from the web and work immediately in one PDF workspace.

Local processing

The app is static and client-side, so documents are not uploaded to Slay PDF servers.

Useful exports

Download all pages, selected pages, separate PDFs, images, text or OCR PDFs.

Online access without the app-server document workflow.

Many online PDF jobs do not require a full account-backed document platform. Slay PDF is built for fast local edits where browser convenience matters but file privacy still matters more.

It is not an enterprise Acrobat replacement. It is a practical alternative for everyday local PDF edits.
