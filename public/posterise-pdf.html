Turn a PDF page into printable poster tiles.

Posterise selected pages into rows and columns, choose paper size and orientation, and export printable PDF tiles from your browser.

Useful for posters, signs and large-format prints.

Slay PDF can split a page into printable tiles when a normal printer is all you have. It also supports page resizing, orientation changes and previewing before export.

The posterise workflow stays in the same local PDF workspace, so you can combine it with cropping, rotation, annotations and page selection.
