Private PDF merger FAQ

Can I merge PDFs privately?

Yes. Slay PDF combines PDFs locally in the browser instead of uploading them to an application server.

Can I remove pages before merging?

Yes. Delete unwanted pages or export selected pages only before downloading the final PDF.

Does Slay PDF save my merged PDF?

No. Your documents and edits stay in this browser. Passwords are never saved.
