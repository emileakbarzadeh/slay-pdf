Secure PDF compressor FAQ

Is Slay PDF a secure PDF compressor?

Slay PDF is designed for local browser processing, so source PDFs are not uploaded to an application server for compression.

Can I delete pages before compressing?

Yes. Remove unwanted pages or export selected pages before creating the compressed PDF.

Where is the compressed PDF created?

The export is generated in your browser and downloaded to your device.
