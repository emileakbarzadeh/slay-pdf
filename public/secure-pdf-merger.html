Secure PDF merger FAQ

Is Slay PDF a secure PDF merger?

Slay PDF is designed for local browser processing, so source PDFs are not uploaded to an application server for merging.

Can I review pages before export?

Yes. You can inspect, reorder and remove pages before downloading the merged PDF.

Where are merged PDFs created?

The export is generated in your browser and downloaded to your device.
