Freelance PDF editor FAQ

Can I sign freelance contracts in Slay PDF?

Yes. You can add a visual signature, text and dates to a PDF. Use a dedicated signing platform when your contract requires identity verification or audit trails.

Can I merge invoices and proposals?

Yes. Import the PDFs, arrange the pages in the order you need and export the merged file locally from your browser.
