Watermarks are often added to drafts, contracts, proof copies and internal documents. Slay PDF keeps that work in the same local editor used for annotation and export.

For stronger privacy workflows, combine watermarking with redaction, password-protected export or selected-page export.

Add watermark to PDF FAQ

Can I add a text watermark?

Yes. Add text in the page editor and position it on the PDF before exporting.

Can I watermark selected pages?

Yes. Edit the pages that need a mark, then export all pages or only selected pages.

Does Slay PDF add its own watermark?

No. Slay PDF is a PDF editor with no app-added watermark on exports.
