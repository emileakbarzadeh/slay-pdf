Local PDF rotator FAQ

Can I rotate only selected PDF pages?

Yes. Select the pages you want to rotate, apply the rotation and export the result.

Can I rotate scans without uploading them?

Yes. Rotation happens in the browser so source files are not sent to a Slay PDF app server.

Can I reorder pages too?

Yes. You can drag pages into a better order before exporting.
